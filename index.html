About Me

Hi there, thankyou for checking my portfolio. I am Antony, a software developer with a particular interest in web technologies, frontend and backend. Focusing on React Js, Material UI, SCSS and CSS on the frontend. On the backend I have built RESTful Apis in Python, Django and the DjangoRestFramework and now considering working with GraphQL Api's. I am also passionate about the world of Data Science and currently I am learning concepts in Data science from cleaning to visualization and modelling.
